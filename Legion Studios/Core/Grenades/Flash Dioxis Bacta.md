All similarly modeled, with the main distinction being the stripe color.

## Flash Grenade
Non lethal grenade that creates a bright flash and loud noise when activated. Affects organics and droids.
### Features
- Works as an ACE flashbang
- Make 9 bang version, Jedi Survivor has one

## Dioxis Grenade
Emits toxic gas, would choke to death organics that stayed in the gas long enough. Used by BX droids

### Features
- Emit green smoke, causing damage over time affect (?)

## Bacta Grenade
Also called "bacta bomb", heals organics over time. **Must take great consideration into balancing, can very easily become way too strong.**

Potentially it could always heal X wounds divided by number of units in the healing area. E.g. it heals 10 wounds over its lifetime, so two people standing in the smoke heal 5 wounds each.

### Features
- Slow heal over time
