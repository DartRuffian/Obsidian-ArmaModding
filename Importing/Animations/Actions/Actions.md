### blends
"Blends": "Arrays that define collections of bones for additive animations, procedural animations, and animation masking. Bones defined in a blend with a weight greater than 0 will be affected by the context in which the blend is used, such as aiming offsets. Bones not defined in a blend will automatically be considered as bones with a weight of 0, and thus will not be influenced.",
"Weights": "Floats from 0 to 1 that define the amount of influence a blend will have over each bone. A value of 0 means no influence, while a value of 1 means maximum influence. Values in between will result in partial influence, with the example of a 90 degree rotation only resulting in a 45 degree rotation if the weight is 0.5."
